SLAY THE SPIRE is a rouguelike video game that challenges players to make it to the top of the spire by building the best deck! Three characters offer their own distinct style of play, keeping players engaged as they have to adapt to the different tactics. Will you burn through and exile all your cards? Hoard them all and sit behind a huge defence? Poke at the enemy and slowly apply poison? Overcome bizzare creatures, risk chance encounters, discover new cards, capture rare relics, and ultimately Slay the Spire!
