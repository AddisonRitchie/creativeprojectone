Arkham Horror

ARKHAM HORROR is a cooperative adventure game themed around H.P. Lovecraft's Cthulhu Mythos. Set in the Roaring Twenties, you and your allied investigators must prevent the appocalypse! No big deal. Upgrade your characters, aquire skills, new allies, interesting items, helpful weapons, and powerful spells. It is up to your team to close the portals opening up around town, or else the Ancient One will rise! If you do happen to awaken the Ancient One...God help us all...
