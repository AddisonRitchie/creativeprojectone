HORIZON ZERO DAWN is an action role-playing game that fufills the dream you never new you had of hunting robots with a bow and arrow. Visually stunning, the game could rely solely on the enjoyment of explorign the open world. Fortunately, the team at Guerrilla Games crafted a compelling story that leads you through the unique world and the mystery of how it came to be. Aloy is the badass female protagonist we've all been waiting for: capable, clever, charismatic, and courageous. This game is a masterpiece, both in gameplay, storyline, and most definitely visually.
